wr ḫrp ḥmw.w

“greatest of directors of craftsmen (Memphite high-priest of Ptah)”, “Oberster Handwerker (Hohepriester des Ptah von Memphis)” (Ward/Fischer, 729).
Usage area: MFR.
Usage period:  Middle Kingdom.
Gender: m.
Bibliography: Maystre 1992; Quirke 2004, 129.
References: TLA: 47660. Hannig: 7829.
Note: Variants: wr ḫrp ḥmw.w nṯr⸗f.
