Cairo CG 20102

Egyptian Museum Cairo CG 20102; JE 11227.
Type: Stela. Subtype: Round-topped stela.
Material: Granite. Size: 860×510 mm.
Text: Private funerary/memorial inscription. Script: Hieroglyphic.
Date: 13th Dyn. (jackals and wḏꜢt-eyes in the round top, S3 for bjtj, the sign Q2)
Provenance:  Abydos (Northern Necropolis).
Origin:  Thebes (the title ḥm-nṯr n jmn).
Owner: jr.j-pꜥ.t; ḥꜣ.tj-ꜥ; jm.j-rꜣ nj.wt; ṯꜣ.tj sꜣ-mnṯ.w.
Bibliography: Lange and Schäfer 1902, 124–5 [s]; Grajetzki 2009, pl. 3 [bp]; seen on display (19/05/2016).
