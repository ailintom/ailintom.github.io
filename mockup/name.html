ptḥ-wnn⸗f

“Ptah exists”, “Ptah existiert” (Ranke, PN I, 139.3–4).
Name types: DN (m)-wnn⸗f, names with Ptah. Usage area: MFR. Usage period: early 12th–mid-13th Dyn. Gender: both.
Bibliography: Ranke 1952, 112–3; Gundacker 2014, 83 n. 187.
TLA: 701626, 701625.
