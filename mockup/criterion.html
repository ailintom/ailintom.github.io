filiation ms.n+M

Unless there are indications of a Late Middle Kingdom date (in which case filiation ms.n+M points to a post 12th Dyn. date), inscriptions with the filiation ms.n+(mother's name) belong to the period from late 11th Dynasty to the end of the reign of Senusret I.
Date:  early 12th Dyn.
